Gentrification in San Francisco 

The dot com boom in the 90s was initally responsible for the increased cost of livining in SF as the influx of tech jobs was not matched with a rapidly increasing housing market to keep prices stable. Over time the government began displacing its residence to make room for its new higher income residents without a place for the lower income residence to move to.

Inflation

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, prices in San Francisco, California were 36.15% higher in 2000 versus 1990 (a $7.23 difference in value). $20 -> $27.23

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, prices in San Francisco, California were 26.44% higher in 2010 versus 2000 (a $5.29 difference in value). $20 -> $25.29

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, prices in San Francisco, California were 31.95% higher in 2020 versus 2010 (a $6.39 difference in value). $20 -> $26.39
